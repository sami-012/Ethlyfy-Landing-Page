What is Ethlyfy?


Ethyl Finance is a world's first ETH-pegged stablecoin protocol backed by various LSTsand LRTs.


Users can mint pETH by overcollateralizing supported LSTs and LRTs, acquiring the principal value at the lowest cost while retaining the yield.

Primary Features

ETH-Pegged

Each pETH is back by LST/LRT and is 1:1 hard pegged to ETH.

Low Risk

Both the Collateral and loan track ETH prices, reducing liquidation risk.

11x Yield

pETH's 110% collateral ratio allows users to leverage their LST/LRT tield upto 11x.
